Activity: Layers in Networking Models
Task:
1) The first part will test your knowledge of the Networking layers. 
To do this, drag a layer from the “Available Items” column and drop it in the correct order in the “Network Layers” column. 
Check your answers. If you make a mistake, click the “Reset” button to try again.

2) In the second part, match samples of the layers to each Networking layer. 
To do this, choose an item from the “Available Items” column and match it with the correct layer in the “Network Layers” column. Check your answers. 
If you make a mistake, click the “Reset” button to try again.
